Expressions by Decor & More, Inc. was founded in 1986 and still exists in its original location in Millersburg, IN. Since our founding, Expressions has been a leading supplier to the home building, manufactured housing and recreational vehicle industries. The Expressions facilities have expanded four different times to meet the ever increasing needs of our business clients. The current facility is filled with over 31,000 square feet of the most unique and affordable home decor merchandise. Our interior designs and home decor have been featured at International Building Shows and received the Excellence in Home Design Award for Outstanding Model Merchandiser.
